Pricing

Pricing should reflect involvement.

Preptus OS™ is designed so pricing can scale with complexity, urgency, and level of support. That keeps the commercial model aligned with the real operating requirement.

Commercial logic

  • Base tier reflects level of implementation involvement
  • Multipliers can account for complexity and risk
  • Diagnostic can remain a low-friction entry point
  • Deeper engagement should feel justified by operating need

Positioning note

The site should avoid price-led language. Instead, pricing is framed as part of a structured implementation model that matches support to operating reality.
